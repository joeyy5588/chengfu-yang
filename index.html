About Me

Hi!
I'm a second year Ph.D. student at UCLA adviced by Prof. Kai-Wei Chang. I previous work as a research assistant in National Taiwan University advised by Prof. Yu-Chiang Frank Wang.

My research interest lies in the intersection of Machine Learning and Robotics, aiming to equip agemts with the ability to reason and interact with the world. Specifically, I am interested in the following topcis:

  • Representation Learning for Decision Making *Vision-and-Language Navigation (VLN), Robotic Manipulation, Generative Models for planning
  • Learning to reason via Embodiment - Learning VL representations via embodied interactions*, *Learning relation-aware VL representations from video
  • Compositionality skills for multimodal generation and reasoning - Open-World Image/Video Captioning, Language-Conditioned Image Manipulation
